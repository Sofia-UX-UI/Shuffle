Core Capabilities

Structured decision-making built for regulated environments

Every capability is designed to satisfy the rigour demanded by compliance teams, external auditors, and governance frameworks — without slowing your teams down.

Decision Records

Every decision is captured as a discrete, versioned record with metadata including owner, status, dates, rationale, and outcome. Fully searchable and filterable across your Confluence workspace.

Unique decision ID & classification
Status workflow (Draft → Approved)
Full version history & diffing
Linked Jira issues & dependencies

Strategy Analysis Matrix

Define multiple strategies per decision and score each against weighted criteria. Visual heatmaps surface the strongest option with quantitative clarity auditors expect.

Real-Time Collaboration

Multiple team members contribute simultaneously. Inline comments, @mentions, and role-based permissions ensure structured input from every stakeholder.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every edit, score change, comment, and approval is timestamped with the user identity. Export audit logs as PDF or CSV for external review.

Compliance Reporting

Generate audit-ready documents in one click. Structured exports meet FCA, PRA, and ISO governance requirements with formatted decision rationale, criteria scores, and approval chains.

How It Works

From question to auditable record in four steps

1

Raise a Decision

Any team member creates a decision record from Confluence or Jira. Context, scope, and stakeholders are captured from day one.

2

Define Strategies & Criteria

Add alternative strategies and define the criteria that matter: cost, risk, compliance, speed, scalability. Weight each criterion by importance.

3

Collaborate & Score

Stakeholders score each strategy against criteria simultaneously. Comments, evidence, and dissenting views are preserved in context.

4

Approve & Archive

Decision owner selects the winning strategy, records the rationale, and locks the record. The full audit trail is export-ready.
